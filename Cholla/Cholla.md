**holla – Full Blueprint**

**1. Purpose \& Vision**



**Name: Cholla**



**Tagline/Meaning: Gives everyone a platform to be heard, to sell, to connect, and to grow. Everyone gets a “voice.”**



**Core Principles: Honesty, community, empowerment, service**



**Goal: Provide a safe, kingdom-aligned, professional platform for commerce, social interaction, and spiritual content without using God’s name directly in branding.**



**2. Core Users \& Target Audience**



**Small-scale sellers \& vendors**



**People without physical stores or formal online presence**



**Can post goods, manage social accounts, and grow followers**



**Buyers / Consumers**



**Looking for local products, services, or spiritual content**



**Delivery Personnel / Connectors**



**Delivery workers for local delivery**



**Connectors: invite users, earn promo-code commissions**



**Spiritual Content Consumers**



**Engage with preloaded messages and videos**



**3. Features \& Functionality**

**Phase 1 – Marketplace \& Social Presence**



**Users can post their goods for sale**



**Ability to connect their social media accounts (FB, Instagram, TikTok, YouTube)**



**Social media growth tools: gain followers, attract audience, advertise products**



**Paid subscription: continued social media management and growth assistance**



**Objective: grow seller presence while providing marketplace**



**Phase 2 – Delivery \& Participation**



**Local delivery network integration**



**Buyers and sellers can hire delivery workers directly**



**Participation system for non-sellers:**



**Invite new users**



**Promo code rewards (% of sales)**



**Dashboard to show earnings, cashable monthly**



**Phase 3 – Offline Messaging**



**Messaging section working without data**



**Upload contacts, chat, connect with users offline**



**Promotes awareness, publicity, and organic growth**



**Phase 4 – Spiritual \& Content Integration**



**YouTube video integration**



**Preloaded spiritual content (e.g., Apostle Joshua Selman messages)**



**Auto-play to encourage engagement**



**Pastors can use all features for free**



**Notification system:**



**Hourly updates for new content**



**Monetization for non-pastor notifications**



**Phase 5 – Local Marketing \& Presence**



**Local advertisement strategy:**



**Flyers distribution at events**



**Vehicle campaigns with music and promotions**



**Collect phone numbers for follow-up**



**Small office for user support**



**Team of ~5 people, paid based on company profit**



**4. Monetization Model**



**Seller Subscriptions – for social media management services**



**Notification Fees – small fee for promotional notifications (non-pastors)**



**Delivery \& Connector Commissions – revenue sharing with users**



**Optional future monetization: premium features, advanced tools**



**5. Content \& Engagement Strategy**



**Blend commerce + social + spiritual content**



**Promote sellers while engaging users through:**



**Social media integration**



**Animations, visuals, or interactive content**



**Spiritual videos and community-focused messaging**



**Encourage referrals, challenges, and engagement programs**



**6. Ethics \& Guidelines**



**No nudity, sexual content, or harassment**



**Users responsible for own actions**



**Clear Terms of Use and content rules**



**Reflects kingdom-aligned, professional values while maintaining impact**



**7. Platform \& Tech Considerations**



**React Native for app development**



**Backend to handle:**



**Marketplace**



**Social media posting \& integration**



**Messaging (online \& offline)**



**Content streaming \& notifications**



**Payment \& dashboard tracking**



**Scalable architecture to allow future growth**



**8. Learning \& Development Approach**



**Every feature learned and practiced contributes directly to building Cholla**



**Hands-on practice:**



**Marketplace functionality**



**Delivery system \& connector tracking**



**Offline messaging \& chat**



**Video streaming \& notifications**



**Social media integration**



**9. Team \& Operational Structure**



**Core team (~5 people initially)**



**Roles flexible and paid from company profit**



**Responsibilities: user support, local marketing, content moderation, analytics tracking**



**10. Key Success Metrics**



**Number of active sellers and buyers**



**Engagement rate (posts, shares, comments)**



**Social media followers growth**



**Delivery \& connector participation**



**Notification interaction rate**



**Revenue from subscriptions and notification fees**



**✅ Cholla Blueprint Summary:**

**Cholla is a multi-phase, integrated platform blending commerce, social presence, local engagement, offline messaging, and spiritual content. Its design prioritizes user empowerment, ethical guidelines, and community growth, with clear monetization streams and measurable success metrics.**

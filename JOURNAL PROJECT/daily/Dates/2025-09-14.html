My daily observations of the Demonic Voices, wins, lessons learned, and reflections with God.

A day in my life

I woke up at 1:32 AM after a vivid dream where I was living with my primary school friend Rasaq and his mom. My presence seemed unnoticed. My aunty Maureen came into the dream and brought bread, which turned into drugs, waking me up. The voice of Ngozi still felt strong, while Iya-the-thief appeared pressured and afraid.
During morning prayer and worship, the voices tried to distract and confuse me, but I remained focused and opened my heart to God. A message and a worship video from my Christian community reinforced my faith, surprising Iya-the-thief and silencing all the voices.
At church service, the spirits attempted attacks through discussion, criticism, praise, insults, advice, and manipulation to implant pride in my mind. I saved my journal on my HTML site, remaining undisturbed despite their interference.
Later, I received a signal to watch a movie. Iya-the-thief tried to intercept, but I followed the guidance without resisting emotions, observing their reactions. I slept and dreamt the voices acknowledged they would be fine as long as I maintained my breathing and focus.

Observations

Attacks through words, feelings, and intuition did not prevail when I released control and trusted God; they bounced back.
The spirits intercept, read, and mimic signals from my mind, aiming to make me think they control me.
I consistently feel God’s presence directing me, especially when I stop trying to control outcomes.
The spirits attempt to predict God’s thoughts and influence my beliefs, but ignoring them renders their attacks ineffective.
When I stay silent and observe, the voices analyze my mind like thieves, discussing and predicting my usual responses.
Key lesson: They often pretend ignorance, acting unaware of what is happening, but their actions reveal their intentions.

Wins

Remained focused on worship and prayer despite distraction and manipulation from the voices.
Saved my journal successfully on my HTML site while resisting spiritual attacks.
Followed spiritual guidance for watching the movie without giving in to fear or control from the spirits.

Lessons Learned

Releasing control and trusting God allows attacks from spirits to bounce back without effect.
Observing quietly and paying attention to patterns in their behavior provides insight into their strategies.
God’s presence is real and protective when I surrender control and maintain focus on Him.

Reflection & Prayer

Lord, thank You for Your guidance and protection today. Teach me to trust You fully, release control, and remain observant of deception. Strengthen me to overcome manipulation and to stay focused on Your presence in all circumstances. Amen.

THU SEP 18 2025

My daily observations of the Demonic Voices, wins, lessons learned, and reflections with God.

A day in my life

I dreamt there was an earthquake and many people died. There was chaos in my secondary school, Aquinas College. First came the earthquake, then a deadly infection spread among the students, killing anyone infected. The police tried to control the outbreak by arresting everyone wearing the school uniform. I hid in a barbershop, and one of the thugs who reported students’ locations to the police came in. As I was about to run out, he signaled to me and whispered, “Go back, don’t go outside—they will catch you.” Then I woke up.
I once had another dream a few days ago about a disaster happening again in my secondary school. After waking, I prayed, studied, and spent my morning reflecting. From 8 AM to 8 PM, I devoted my time to studying and meditating on the Word of God.

Observations

The voices are drawing back—they don’t attack as before. It feels like they’re holding back, possibly because I’m observing and journaling about them.
They try to make me believe they are still in charge, but I sense their weakness and fear.
They insult me, make offers for me to accept them, but I keep refusing and rebuking them in Jesus’ name.
They seem pressured and uncomfortable, pretending to be strong through small temptations and attacks.
I noticed they get hungry often, making me feel hungry repeatedly—as though trying to manipulate me to eat more.
They threaten that I’ll die if I keep journaling about them, but I will not stop.

Wins

I continued journaling and documenting my experiences without fear.
I stayed consistent in studying and praying from morning till night.
I resisted all their threats, lies, and manipulations and focused on God’s Word.

Lessons Learned

The enemy becomes weaker when I pay attention, observe, and write down what’s happening.
Discerning their tricks and rebuking in Jesus’ name breaks their influence.
Journaling strengthens awareness and makes me more spiritually alert and bold.

Reflection & Prayer

Lord Jesus, thank You for keeping me aware and strong. Thank You for revealing the enemy’s weakness and giving me peace through Your Word. Help me to remain faithful, observant, and fearless as I continue documenting these experiences with You by my side. Amen.

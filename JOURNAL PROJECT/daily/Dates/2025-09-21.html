SUN SEP 21 2025

5:32 AM

Dream

I saw myself in the middle of a T-junction where armed robbers were attacking us. Suddenly, some ladies with guns came and chased them away. Just when we thought we were safe, a gang wearing red and black appeared and ordered us to march toward the king’s palace.

As we marched, a voice whispered to me, “Don’t enter that palace, they will kill you all and use you for sacrifice.” I managed to escape before reaching the palace. The next day, those who entered the palace were paraded around the city, meant to be used for sacrifice. People watched helplessly. Then a brave girl challenged the gang and was struck by them. She called her father, who was an army general, and he came with soldiers to rescue the people.

Day Summary

Yesterday at church, for the first time, I wore a shirt, tie, trousers, and black shoes — a different look from my usual hip-hop style with baggy jeans and Timberlands. The voices mocked me, saying I looked like a clown. I had also stopped using the cream that made my skin fair, deciding to remain dark and natural.

They mocked me again, calling me names, but I stood firm. I decided to keep dressing modestly and burn my old street clothes. I want to look different — simple, decent, and focused on God. I thought maybe the voices liked my old look, and now that I’ve changed, they’re losing interest in me. These days, they feel far away — they mostly fight among themselves, shouting, cursing, and accusing one another. They even beg me to talk and make peace with them, promising not to harm me if I align with them, but I refuse.

Key Observations

  • They fight among themselves these days.
  • Whatever I repeatedly say or do seems to become automatic in their realm.
  • They depend on “Baba the Thief” to bring them back to order — without him, they are confused and visionless.

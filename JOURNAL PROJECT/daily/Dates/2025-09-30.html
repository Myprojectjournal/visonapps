TUE SEP 30 2025

Daily journal — prayer, warfare notes, creative sparks, and reflections

Morning

I woke up, listened to the Word of God, prayed and sang praises. Afterward I studied for two hours and successfully kept my boundary — I didn’t break it. I deleted and found a way to block distracting apps like YouTube, which the demonic voices often use against me. My brother traveled back to school today.

Episodes

- They tried to negotiate, offering the whole world, but I rebuked them.
- Their aggression rose; they tried to subdue me with fear, but I stood firm and rebuked them.
- They mocked, insulted, and used deceitful tactics to distract me and steal creative visions. I resisted through prayer and wisdom.

Key Observations

  • They negotiate with offers of wealth and power — I rebuked them and stood strong.
  • They grew more aggressive but their aggression failed to break my faith and boundaries.
  • Insults, mockery, and deception are their primary weapons; words are their greatest tool.
  • They attempt to intercept my thoughts and claim my creative ideas as theirs — I used God’s wisdom to reclaim them.
  • Without words (deception), their power weakens — their jurisdiction and voice are limited.
  • They can try to blur vision or cause physical distraction, but discipline and prayer counteract this.
  • I received a creative spark to build a Guardian App — a protective, guiding tool for others (and for my own focus).
  • God reminded me of the central role Scripture plays in growth and protection for believers.

Reflection Verse

“Submit yourselves, then, to God. Resist the devil, and he will flee from you.” — James 4:7

Final Note

Peace came from practical steps: prayer, boundaries, blocking distractions, and holding to study. Creativity returned as a God-given idea — the Guardian App. I will keep journaling and building, trusting God to guard and guide each step.

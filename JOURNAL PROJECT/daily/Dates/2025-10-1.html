WED OCT 1 2025

Daily journal — growth, peace, divine guidance, and lessons from God

Morning

I woke up by 4:25 and it was raining. The demonic voices tried to distract me and made me dress in an ungodly way, preventing me from praying and singing praises to God. Later, I went to the shop with my dad. Around 11:45, I began studying and was graced with understanding — I finished jQuery and began JavaScript with ChatGPT. Afterward, I prayed the Hail Mary and went to choir.

Evening

My father fell sick and called me to return to help him close the store. I went home while he waited for my mother, who was traveling back to town. I cooked jollof rice, and we ate together. My brother received a reply for his internship, and while discussing it, my second brother lashed out at us because he was on a call. I got angry but didn’t say anything, choosing to correct that attitude next time.

Key Observations

  • The demonic voices seemed weaker this morning when I woke up.
  • They no longer argue or speak with the same strength as before.
  • Setting boundaries and letting God’s will lead brings me peace and understanding.
  • I don’t get emotional or try to justify people’s wrong actions anymore.
  • I now put God first above everything and value myself rightly.
  • I set boundaries but let God decide their outcome — not my emotions.
  • I’m becoming spiritually wise and learning to discern God’s will.
  • These days, I feel more peaceful, happy, and grateful.
  • Anger and emotional trauma no longer overpower me.
  • When I decide to act in line with God’s will, the start may be hard, but the Spirit empowers me to finish easily.

Reflection Verse

“Trust in the Lord with all your heart and lean not on your own understanding; in all your ways submit to Him, and He will make your paths straight.” — Proverbs 3:5–6

Final Note

Today marked a quiet spiritual victory — less noise, more grace. By letting go of control and allowing God to decide, I found rest for my soul. Boundaries, patience, and divine wisdom are shaping my walk with God.

THUR OCT 2 2025

Daily journal — setting spiritual boundaries and walking with God

Morning & Afternoon

I woke up and didn’t go to the shop today. I stayed at home and tried to study, but the demonic voices distracted me and made me eat instead. I didn’t fight it this time — I allowed God to lead me. After eating, I felt like watching a movie, so I watched two. When I decided to rest afterward, a sudden wave of urges for drugs came strongly upon me.

Evening

I immediately began to pray and praise God. As I prayed, I started setting boundaries again, and the urge disappeared completely. I felt peace return to my heart and my emotions became stable.

To strengthen my boundaries, I decided to stay around people and avoid being alone. I left my room, stayed in my brother’s room, and later went to church for choir practice. The demonic voices tried to attack me again with love emotions, but I conquered them through prayer and focus on God.

Key Observations

  • Praying and thanking God restores strength and clears boundaries set by weakness.
  • Boundaries guided by God’s wisdom weaken emotional and spiritual attacks.
  • The Word and Power of God are their greatest threat.
  • Their influence and effect over me have greatly weakened.

Reflection Verse

“Submit yourselves therefore to God. Resist the devil, and he will flee from you.” — James 4:7

Final Note

Today was a quiet test of discipline and trust. I learned again that when I let God lead — not my fear or my will — He gives peace that silences every storm. The urge faded, but His strength remained.
